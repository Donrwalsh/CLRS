> Suppose that all characters in the pattern $$P$$ are different. Show how to accelerate NAIVE-STRING-MATCHER to run in time $$O(n)$$ on an $$n$$-character text $$T$$.

NAIVE-STRING-MATCHER-FOR-DISTINCT-PATTERN(T, P)

01 `n = T.length`

02 `m = P.length`

03 `p_position = 0`

04 `for s = 0 to n - 1:`

05 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `if T[s] == P[p_position]:`

06 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `if p_position == m - 1:`

07 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `print "Pattern occurs with shift" s - m + 1`

08 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `p_position = 0`

09 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `else:`

10 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `p_position++`

11 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `else:`

12 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `p_position = 0`

13 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `if T[s] == P[p_position]:`

14 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; `p_poisiton++`;
